AI-Powered Brain MRI Tumor Detection & Clinical Decision Support Platform

Project Overview

NeuraSight is an advanced medical imaging platform that leverages deep learning and explainable AI to detect and classify brain tumors from MRI scans. The system provides clinical decision support through high-accuracy predictions, visual explanations via Grad-CAM heatmaps, and comprehensive diagnostic insights.

This final year project demonstrates the application of transfer learning, specifically using EfficientNet-B0 architecture, to achieve state-of-the-art performance in medical image classification while maintaining interpretability through explainable AI techniques.

Model Architecture

EfficientNet-B0

The system employs EfficientNet-B0 as the backbone architecture, chosen for its optimal balance between accuracy and computational efficiency. EfficientNet uses compound scaling to uniformly scale network width, depth, and resolution, achieving superior performance with fewer parameters.

Key Features:

  • Transfer Learning from ImageNet
  • Fine-tuned for Medical Imaging
  • Efficient Compound Scaling
  • Mobile-Friendly Architecture

Explainable AI

Grad-CAM Visualization

Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping (Grad-CAM) is implemented to provide visual explanations for model predictions. This technique highlights the regions of the MRI scan that most influenced the classification decision, enabling clinicians to understand and validate the AI's reasoning.

Benefits:

  • Visual Interpretation of Predictions
  • Identifies Suspicious Regions
  • Builds Clinical Trust
  • Supports Decision Making

Future Expansion

The NeuraSight platform is designed with extensibility in mind. Future development plans include expanding the system to support additional medical imaging modalities and diagnostic applications:

  • CT Scan Analysis: Extend deep learning models to analyze computed tomography scans for various pathologies including lung nodules and abdominal abnormalities.
  • X-Ray Diagnostics: Implement chest X-ray analysis for pneumonia detection, COVID-19 screening, and other pulmonary conditions.
  • Retinal Imaging: Develop models for diabetic retinopathy detection and age-related macular degeneration screening from fundus photographs.
  • Skin Lesion Detection: Create dermatological AI for melanoma and skin cancer detection from dermoscopic images.
  • Multi-Modal Fusion: Integrate multiple imaging modalities for comprehensive diagnostic insights and improved accuracy.
  • Real-Time Inference: Optimize models for edge deployment and real-time clinical decision support in hospital environments.

Ethical Considerations

This system is designed as a clinical decision support tool, not a replacement for professional medical judgment. All predictions should be validated by qualified healthcare professionals. The explainable AI component ensures transparency in the decision-making process, supporting informed clinical decisions.
